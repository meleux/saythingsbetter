Hi, I’m Lila Smith, and I want to help you
Say Things Better.

Using my own theater-based Intentional Communication methodology, I help entrepreneurs and business leaders to identify and express their true brand value. I help them speak to the people who need to hear their message, in the way they’ll BEST be able to receive it.

This lights up content marketing strategy, LinkedIn profiles, pitch language, customer service templates and guidelines, and the internal policies that empower employees to become authentic ambassadors.

My tools are also helpful untricking tricky interpersonal and professional communication!

Ever find yourself saying "it was never my intention to offend you"? Next time, ask yourself if you know what your intention WAS, not just what it wasn't.
